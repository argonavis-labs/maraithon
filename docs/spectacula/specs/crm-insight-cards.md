# CRM Insight Cards

Status: Done
Purpose: Add a dedicated Insights section to the authenticated operator navigation that surfaces CRM cleanup and relationship suggestions as compact review cards.

## Current State

Maraithon already has:

- `Maraithon.Crm` with user-scoped people, contact details, relationship fields, merge support, merge audit records, person links, and CRM observations.
- A `/operator/people` LiveView for searching active CRM people, opening a person detail panel, assigning relationship context, deleting contacts, and manually merging selected duplicates.
- `Maraithon.Insights` for persisted actionable insights generated by agents. Those cards currently render inside `DashboardLive`.
- A Catalyst-aligned sidebar/mobile navigation shell owned by `MaraithonWeb.AdminNavigation`.

There is no dedicated Insights page in the navigation, and no first-party CRM insight generator that scans CRM people for duplicates or inferred relationship context.

## Goals

- Add an authenticated `/insights` LiveView and expose it as its own navbar item.
- Render CRM insight cards in a calm, row-oriented operational layout that follows `DESIGN.md`.
- Detect likely duplicate CRM people from exact normalized display-name matches and overlapping contact details.
- Detect likely relationship suggestions from CRM people, notes, links, and observations when there is evidence for relationship phrases such as wife, daughter, son, or mother-in-law.
- Require operator confirmation before writing a relationship suggestion into CRM.
- Keep merge suggestions review-first: link to the People page with an appropriate search instead of auto-merging from the Insights page.

## Non-Goals

- No LLM call in the request path.
- No new database table for dismissed CRM insights.
- No automatic destructive merge from the Insights page.
- No broad relationship ontology beyond a small first-pass set of family relationship labels.
- No removal of the existing dashboard actionable-insight section.

## UX Contract

Route: `/insights`

Layout:

- The LiveView must begin with `<Layouts.app flash={@flash} current_path={@current_path} current_user={@current_user}>`.
- Page title: `Insights`.
- Page subtitle: short, direct copy describing CRM cleanup and relationship review.
- Top summary strip with counts for total CRM insights, duplicate suggestions, and relationship suggestions.
- Main content as two compact sections:
  - `CRM cleanup`: possible duplicate people or records that can be combined.
  - `Relationship suggestions`: relationship labels Maraithon can apply after confirmation.

Duplicate card:

| Element | Behavior |
| --- | --- |
| Title | `Possible duplicate: <name>` |
| Summary | Names and evidence for why the records may represent the same person |
| Evidence | Contact overlap, exact name match, or same resolved participant evidence |
| Action | `Review in People`, navigating to `/operator/people?q=<search>` |

Relationship card:

| Element | Behavior |
| --- | --- |
| Title | `Review relationship: <person> as your <relationship>` |
| Summary | Evidence-backed explanation that tells the user to confirm before changing the CRM record |
| Evidence | One or more snippets from person notes, person links, or CRM observations |
| Primary action | `Apply relationship`, updating the CRM person relationship and structured family metadata for family roles |
| Secondary action | `Review person`, navigating to the person detail panel |
| Quiet action | `Hide for now`, hiding the card for the current LiveView session only |

Empty state:

- If no CRM insights are found, show a short message that the CRM looks clean for now and link to `/operator/people`.

## Backend Contract

Add `Maraithon.Crm.Insights` as a pure CRM insight generator:

- `list_for_user(user_id, opts \\ [])` returns a map:
  - `duplicate_suggestions`
  - `relationship_suggestions`
  - `total_count`
- The function is user-scoped and returns empty lists for invalid user ids.
- Limit defaults should keep the UI bounded: 100 active people, 250 recent observations, and 25 total suggestions per category.
- Relationship suggestions must not overwrite a non-blank person relationship.
- Suggestions must include stable ids, type, title, summary, confidence, evidence, and action metadata.

Duplicate rules:

- Normalize names by lowercasing, trimming punctuation, and collapsing whitespace.
- Suggest duplicates for two or more active people that share the same normalized display name.
- Suggest duplicates for two or more active people that share at least one email, phone, Slack id, or Telegram id.
- Choose the People page search query from the most readable display name in the candidate group.

Relationship rules:

- Scan person notes, person link text fields, and recent CRM observation subject/excerpt/metadata text involving a person id.
- First pass relationship vocabulary:
  - `wife`
  - `husband`
  - `spouse`
  - `daughter`
  - `son`
  - `mother-in-law`
  - `father-in-law`
- Detect direct phrases such as `<name> is my wife`, `<name> is your wife`, `my wife <name>`, `your daughter <name>`, and `mother in law <name>`.
- Normalize hyphenation for in-law labels in output.
- Confidence is deterministic and bounded:
  - high when the phrase includes the person display name and a relationship label.
  - medium when the evidence is attached to the person but does not include the full display name.
- Evidence snippets must be short and source-labeled.

Relationship apply behavior:

- The LiveView handles `apply_relationship_suggestion`.
- It validates that the suggestion id still exists for the current user before writing.
- It calls `Maraithon.Crm.upsert_person/2` with the person id, display name, relationship label, and metadata:
  - `relationship_context_source: "crm_insights"`
  - `relationship_context_updated_at`
  - `relationship_suggestion_id`
  - `relationship_suggestion_confidence`
- On success, the card disappears because the person now has a relationship.
